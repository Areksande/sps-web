Special Program in Sports

Building Champions On and Off the Field Through Discipline, Passion, and Excellence.

Introduction

The Taal High School supports the Department of Education’s mission of providing quality education while promoting sports as an essential part of holistic youth development. In school year 2024–2025, the school initiated the Special Program in Sports (SPS) to support talented students in various sports disciplines. This accomplishment report summarizes the key activities, achievements, and outcomes of the program during the covered period.

Program Description

The Special Program in Sports (SPS) was originally conceived to address the needs of talented students in different sports disciplines and to identify and create a pool of athletes who will be trained to compete in Regional and National Competitions.

Program Objectives

  • To enhance athletic potential
  • To prepare learners to work or higher learning
  • To achieve school – sport balance
  • Prepare athletes for high-level Regional and National competitions.
